# Go Bananas

**Banana ripeness, on-device.** Coming soon to the App Store and Google Play.

Go Bananas tells you when a banana is at its peak. Point your camera — the app reads the color of the peel and gives you a stage rating (from "not yet" to "past its prime") plus a 5-banana rating. The bunch feature lets you plan 5–8 virtual bananas so the last one doesn't go to waste.

Everything runs on your phone. No upload, no account, no tracking.

🔬 How It Works
For Photos:
  1. Upload a clear photo of your banana
  2. Our AI extracts the dominant color
  3. Color is mapped to ripeness stage
  4. Get detailed analysis and recommendations
For Color Picker:
  1. Select the color that matches your banana
  2. Color is converted to hue value
  3. Hue is mapped to ripeness stage
  4. Get instant ripeness estimation
